A Painted Fence is..

Truly transformative

Does lively landscaping matter if everything around it is a lifeless gray? By changing the color of your fence to something beautiful and vibrant, you can breathe new life into the atmosphere your backyard creates.

Shielded from efflorescence

Our painting process treats your fence with a sealant designed to keep efflorescence - the ugly white powder you've seen accumulated on outdoor walls - away, preventing moisture from slipping through the cracks. As a result, your yard will persist in looking hygienic and fresh.

Extremely affordable

Getting your fence painted will make your backyard feel brand new at a fraction of the cost. We use a simple pricing model based on the size and type of your fence, and we hand out free quotes like candy on Halloween.

Effortlessly long-lasting

The work that we do will last for 4-6 years without any maintenance. We offer a three year grace period: at any point during that time period, if you notice a deterioration of paint quality due to normal wear and tear, we'll come and touch it up for you for free.
