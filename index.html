4 Good Reasons

to get your fence painted.

Truly transformative

Lively landscaping doesn't matter if everything around it is a lifeless gray. By changing the color of your fence to something beautiful and vibrant, you can breathe new life into the atmosphere your backyard creates, and make those plants pop!

Exceptionally affordable

The cost of getting your fence painted is fraction of other backyard renovations. We use a simple pricing model based on the size and type of your fence, and we hand out free quotes like candy on Halloween!

Effortlessly long-lasting

The work that we do will last for 4-6 years without maintenance. We offer a three year grace period: at any point during that time, if you notice a deterioration of paint quality due to normal wear and tear, we'll touch it up for free!

Shielded from efflorescence

Our painting process treats your fence with a sealant specifically designed to keep away efflorescence - the ugly white powder you've seen built up on outdoor walls. As a result, your fence will continue looking hygienic and fresh!
