How it Works

1 Making Contact

Get in touch with us using the contact form at the bottom of this page, and we will reach out to you within 24 hours!

You can also reach us directly by emailing us at contact@freshfences.com or giving us a call at (480) 492-5279.

2 Quoting the Job

At a time convenient for you, we’ll come to your home to measure your fence, talk color options, and answer any questions you might have.

Before we go, you’ll have a quote in your hand and a date scheduled for us to come back!

3 Doing the Work

  1. We will reach out to you the day of the job to confirm our arrival time.
  2. We prep the fence and yard using tape and drop cloths to cover anything that shouldn't change color.
  3. We coat the fence in a special primer that helps the paint stick easier and last longer.
  4. We paint the fence in 1-2 even coats, determined primarily by the existing fence color and the color that is being painted.
  5. We clean up our prep work with a goal to leave the yard better than we found it.
  6. We review the work with the homeowner and collect payment, accepting card or cash.
  7. We collect a digital signature and send a receipt via email.
  8. We leave the homeowner with extra paint for any touch up needed over time.
