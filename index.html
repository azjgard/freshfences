Here's how it works..

1 Getting in Touch

Get in touch with us using the contact form at the bottom of this page. Send us your name, your city, and a way we can get a hold of you. We will reach out to you within 24 hours.

You can also reach us directly by emailing us at contact@freshfences.com or giving us a call at (480) 492-5279.

2 Quoting the Work

At a time convenient for you, we’ll come to your home to measure your fence, talk color options, and answer any questions you might have.

Before we go, you’ll have a price in your hand and a date scheduled for us to come back and get the work done.

3 Doing the Job

The day of the job, we will reach out to give you a rundown of our schedule and confirm our arrival time.

Most jobs take 2-3 hours. Before we go, we'll clean everything up, pack up our equipment, and send you a digital invoice to collect your payment and signature. Easy as pie!
